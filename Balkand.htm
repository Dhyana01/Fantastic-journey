Bala Kandam, meaning "Book of Childhood," is the initial book of the Ramayana, traditionally attributed to the sage Valmiki. It covers the divine birth and early life of Rama, the prince of Ayodhya, along with his brothers Bharata, Lakshmana, and Shatrughna.
