The Spice Route Skewers (Plant-Based Option):

The Sultan's Tofu: Large cubes of firm, pressed tofu and chunky vegetables (peppers, zucchini, red onion) marinated overnight in a rich, smoky harissa and preserved lemon paste. They are flash-grilled to achieve a crispy exterior and served with a cooling, mint-infused dipping sauce.

Aetherial Doughnuts (Vegan Selection):

The Celestial Rings: A lighter, yeast-risen doughnut made with a plant-based batter. These are often flavored with seasonal fruits and spices, such as Cardamom-Orange Blossom or a rich Blueberry-Basil. They are dusted with natural fruit powders for vibrant color.

The Fortune Teller's Fryery (Vegan Favorite):

The Mystic Mushroom Fries: Crispy-fried king oyster mushroom "steaks" cut into thick, satisfying strips, resembling the texture of meat. They are seasoned with a bold Smoked Paprika and Rosemary blend and served with a side of creamy, homemade Avocado-Lime Crema (vegan).

The Sugar Sculpture Stand (Naturally Vegan Options):

Many of our famous rock candy gems, candied fruits, and caramelized apples are naturally vegan. The specialty is the "Emerald Apple," a large granny smith apple dipped in a smooth, vibrant green caramel infused with Matcha Green Tea, often topped with toasted coconut flakes.
