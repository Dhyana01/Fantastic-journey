The seven kands of ramayana

The Seven Kandas in the Natyashastra refer to the major divisions of the Ramayana, which is an epic attributed to Valmiki. This structure consists of seven distinct sections: Balakanda, Ayodhyakanda, Aranyakanda, Kishkindhakanda, Sundarakanda, Yuddhakanda, and Uttarakanda.
